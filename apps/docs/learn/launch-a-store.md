# Launch a store

Everything to go from no store to taking orders. Commerce is a paid feature —
see [Billing & plans](/workspace-and-billing/billing-and-plans/overview).

1. **[Commerce overview](/commerce-and-bookings/commerce/overview)** — what the
   storefront includes: catalog, orders, shipping, taxes, and your own Stripe
   account.
2. **[Build the catalog](/commerce-and-bookings/commerce/catalog)** — products,
   options and variants, categories, and collections.
3. **[Commerce end to end](/guides/commerce-end-to-end)** — the full guide:
   storefront blocks, checkout for one-time and subscription sales, and running
   orders from the console.
4. **[POS & reservations](/commerce-and-bookings/commerce/pos-and-reservations)** —
   sell in person and take date-range reservations.
5. **[Bookings & scheduling](/commerce-and-bookings/bookings/overview)** — offer
   services with availability and reminders.
