# Automate your site

Make your site do things on its own — send an email on a form submission, sync
an order to another system, or run a multi-step pipeline.

1. **[Workflows, actions & webhooks](/marketing-and-automation/workflows-and-actions/overview)** —
   the automation model: triggers, actions, and workflows.
2. **[Actions builder](/marketing-and-automation/workflows-and-actions/actions-builder)** —
   map a single event to a single action.
3. **[Build a workflow](/marketing-and-automation/workflows-and-actions/build-a-workflow)** —
   chain multiple steps on a site event.
4. **[Webhooks](/marketing-and-automation/workflows-and-actions/webhooks)** —
   connect external systems in and out ([API webhooks reference](/api/integrations/webhooks)).
5. **[Email campaigns](/marketing-and-automation/email-campaigns/overview)** —
   reach audiences built from your contacts.
6. **[Marketing overlays](/marketing-and-automation/marketing-overlays/overview)** —
   announcement bars and promotional popups with triggers.
